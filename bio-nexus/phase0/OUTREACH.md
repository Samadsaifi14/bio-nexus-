## Cold Outreach Template — For Students

**Subject:** 15 min — struggling with BLAST / protein analysis?

Hey [Name],

I'm a fellow M.Sc. bioinformatics student building a tool that makes protein sequence analysis instant — paste a sequence, get a complete interpreted result without touching NCBI, UniProt, or any command line.

I'd love 15 minutes to understand:
1. What do you do when you need to analyze a protein sequence?
2. What's the most frustrating step?
3. Would instant AI-powered results help?

This is purely a research conversation — no sales pitch, no demo. Just trying to build something useful for people like us.

Want to hop on a quick call this week?

Best,
Samad (you are yourself)
Bio Nexus

---

## Call Script (15 minutes)

### Opening (1 min)
- "I'm building a bioinformatics tool and want to make sure I'm solving real problems real students face. Not a sales call — I want your honest frustration."

### The One Question (10 min)
- **"Walk me through the last time you tried to analyze a biological sequence."**

Follow-ups (only if needed):
- What tools did you open first?
- What went wrong or was confusing?
- How long did it take from start to having an answer?
- Did you ask someone for help? Who?

### Closing (4 min)
- "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about that process, what would it be?"
- "Is there anyone else in your class/lab who has the same problem?"
- "Want early access to try the prototype?"

### Post-Call
- Log feedback in TRACKER.md immediately
- Add to Beta Users list if interested
- Send a thank-you text within an hour
